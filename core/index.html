Delegation Oversight

Core Responsibilities & Activities

Day to day, the Delegation Oversight team performs a variety of duties to fulfill our mission. Below you will find some of our core responsibilities and how they are carried out in practice:

Performance Monitoring

We continually review performance metrics from our delegates to ensure they meet established benchmarks and standards.

Routine Communication & Meetings

We conduct regular oversight meetings, often called Joint Operations Committees (JOCs), to discuss performance results, address issues, and coordinate improvements with delegates.

Audits & Site Visits

Annually, we perform comprehensive audits of each delegate to verify compliance with KHS policies, regulatory standards, staff qualifications, and data‑security practices.

Contract Management

Our department supports the responsible business in creating service level agreements and establishing key performance indicators to ensure the delegated partner is held responsible for the delegated functions.

Interdepartmental Collaboration

We collaborate closely with all KHS departments and work closely with the KHS Compliance Department—to provide comprehensive oversight and effective responses to any delegate‑related issues, complaints, or questions.
